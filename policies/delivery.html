This Delivery Policy explains how delivery and pick-up work when you order from Burger King Ghana online.

1. Delivery Zones

We currently deliver to major areas across Accra, Tema, and Kumasi. Our delivery zones are expanding over time. Enter your delivery address at checkout to confirm whether we currently deliver to your location — if your address falls outside our delivery zone, the website will let you know and you can choose pick-up from your nearest branch instead.

2. Delivery Times

Our average delivery time is 25 to 35 minutes from order confirmation, depending on your distance from the nearest branch and current order demand. During peak periods (lunchtime, evenings, weekends, and public holidays), delivery may take longer than usual. Delivery time estimates shown on the website and in your order confirmation are estimates, not guaranteed delivery windows.

3. Pick-up Orders

If you choose pick-up at checkout, your order will be prepared at your selected branch and held for collection. You will receive a notification when your order is ready. To collect your order, staff will ask for your order reference number or the phone number used to place the order — please have one of these ready.

4. Tracking Your Order

After placing your order, you can track its status from your order confirmation page using your order reference number. Order status moves through Pending → Confirmed → Preparing → Ready for Pick-up or Out for Delivery → Completed. If you have any concerns about your order's status, contact us directly using the details below.

5. Delivery Issues

If your order arrives incomplete, incorrect, or significantly later than the estimated time, contact us immediately so we can resolve it — including arranging a replacement, correction, or refund where appropriate. See our Refund Policy for how refunds for delivery issues are handled. If a delivery address is incomplete, inaccessible, or no one is available to receive the order after reasonable attempts to contact you, we reserve the right to treat the order as undeliverable, in which case our Refund Policy on undeliverable orders will apply.
